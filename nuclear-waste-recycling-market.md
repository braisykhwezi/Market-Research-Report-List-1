<p><p>The nuclear waste recycling market encompasses several methods for managing spent nuclear fuel and radioactive waste. Direct disposal involves burying waste in deep geological formations to isolate it from the environment. Underwater storage temporarily contains waste in deep water bodies to reduce radiation exposure and facilitate monitoring.
