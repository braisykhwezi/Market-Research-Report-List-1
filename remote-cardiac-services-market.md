<p><p>The Remote Cardiac Services market encompasses various monitoring devices that facilitate patient health management from home. Heart monitors track cardiac activity, aiding in the detection of irregularities. ECG devices provide electrocardiogram assessments remotely, while blood pressure monitors manage hypertension through consistent tracking. Breath monitors assess respiratory function, crucial for patients with cardiac conditions.
