<p><p>The construction management system market is divided into two primary types: on-premise and cloud-based solutions. On-premise systems are installed locally on a company's hardware, offering greater control and customization but requiring significant upfront investment and ongoing maintenance. In contrast, cloud-based solutions are hosted online, allowing for easier access, collaboration, and scalability. They often operate on a subscription model, reducing upfront costs and enabling real-time updates and data sharing among stakeholders. Each type serves different organizational needs and preferences.</p></p>
