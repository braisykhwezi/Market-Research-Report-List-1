<p><strong>The Polyurethane Fiber Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Solution Dry Spinning</li><li>Solution Wet Spinning</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The polyurethane fiber market can be categorized primarily into three types based on manufacturing processes: solution dry spinning, solution wet spinning, and others. Solution dry spinning involves dissolving polymer in a solvent, then evaporating the solvent during fiber formation, resulting in high strength fibers. Solution wet spinning requires the polymer solution to be extruded into a coagulation bath to solidify, creating flexible fibers suitable for various applications. "Others" encompasses alternative methods and emerging technologies in fiber production, broadening market diversity.</p></p>
