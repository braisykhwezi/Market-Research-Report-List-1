<p><p>The capacitive touch controllers market is segmented into multi-touch and single-touch types. Multi-touch (mutual capacitive) controllers can detect multiple touch points simultaneously, enabling gestures and complex interactions, making them ideal for smartphones and tablets. Single-touch (self-capacitive) controllers, on the other hand, handle one touch point at a time, often used in simpler applications like basic home appliances. Additionally, the 'others' category includes specialized controllers catering to niche markets or specific applications, expanding the overall versatility of touch technology.</p></p>
