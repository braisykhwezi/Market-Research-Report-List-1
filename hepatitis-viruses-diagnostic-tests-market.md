<p><p>The hepatitis viruses diagnostic tests market includes various methodologies for detecting viral infections. Transcription-Mediated Amplification (TMA) amplifies viral RNA for sensitive detection, while Polymerase Chain Reaction (PCR) amplifies DNA to detect viral presence accurately. Other methods may include rapid tests and point-of-care diagnostics, catering to diverse diagnostic needs in hepatitis management.</p></p>
