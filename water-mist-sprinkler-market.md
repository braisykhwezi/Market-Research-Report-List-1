<p><strong>The Water Mist Sprinkler Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Wet Pipe System</li><li>Dry Pipe System</li><li>Deluge System</li><li>Preaction System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The water mist sprinkler market encompasses several systems designed for fire suppression. The wet pipe system uses permanently filled pipes with water, offering immediate response. The dry pipe system contains pressurized air or gas, releasing water only when activated. The deluge system features open heads that discharge large volumes of water for rapid fire control. The preaction system combines elements of wet and dry systems, requiring a two-step activation to allow water flow, thus minimizing accidental discharge. Each type serves distinct fire protection needs.</p></p>
