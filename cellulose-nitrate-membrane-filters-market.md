Those with pore sizes ranging from 0.45 to 6 µm are used for particle retention and clarification in various applications like microbiology and analytical testing. Filters with pore sizes above 6 µm cater to larger particles, commonly used in water treatment and industrial processes, where filtration of coarse materials is necessary.</p></p>
