<p><strong>The Sleep Therapy Devices Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>CPAP (Continuous Positive Airway Pressure)</li><li>BiPAP (Bilevel Positive Airway Pressure)</li><li>VPAP (Variable Positive Airway Pressure)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The sleep therapy devices market includes various types catering to sleep apnea treatment. CPAP (Continuous Positive Airway Pressure) devices deliver a steady air pressure to keep airways open during sleep. BiPAP (Bilevel Positive Airway Pressure) offers two pressure settings for inhalation and exhalation, making it easier for users with higher resistance. VPAP (Variable Positive Airway Pressure) adjusts the pressure throughout the night based on breathing patterns, enhancing comfort and efficacy. Each device serves specific patient needs, enhancing sleep quality and health outcomes.</p></p>
