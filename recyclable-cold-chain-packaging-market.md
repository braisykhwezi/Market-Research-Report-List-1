<p><p>The recyclable cold chain packaging market encompasses various materials tailored for temperature-sensitive goods. Corrugated cardboard is lightweight and eco-friendly, offering insulation while being easily recyclable. Expanded polystyrene (EPS) provides superior thermal protection, retaining temperature effectively but poses recycling challenges. Polyurethane (PUR) is known for its excellent insulation properties, making it suitable for high-performance applications, yet its recyclability can be limited. Other materials can include biodegradable options, demonstrating a growing trend towards sustainable solutions in cold chain logistics.</p></p>
