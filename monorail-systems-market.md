<p><strong>The Monorail Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Straddle Type</li><li>Suspended Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Monorail systems are categorized into two main market types: straddle type and suspended type. Straddle type monorails feature a single beam that supports the train above, allowing for efficient ground-level clearance and minimal land disruption. They are commonly used for urban transit. In contrast, suspended type monorails hang below a beam, offering a unique aerial perspective and reducing the impact of ground structures. Both types provide eco-friendly transportation solutions with lower operational costs and reduced traffic congestion in urban settings.</p></p>
