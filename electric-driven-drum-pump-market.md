<p><p>The electric-driven drum pump market comprises various pump types, including gear pumps, screw pumps, and vane pumps. Gear pumps utilize meshing gears to move fluids, providing high pressure and consistent flow rates, ideal for viscous liquids. Screw pumps employ helical screws to convey fluids, offering high efficiency and the ability to handle abrasive materials. Vane pumps consist of rotating vanes that expand and contract to create suction, delivering smooth operation and variable flow rates, suitable for various applications in fluid transfer.</p></p>
