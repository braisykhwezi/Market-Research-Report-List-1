<p><p>The engine management sensors market comprises several critical components that enhance engine performance and efficiency. Camshaft and crankshaft position sensors monitor the positioning of engine components, ensuring optimal timing. Oxygen sensors measure exhaust gases to regulate fuel-air mixtures. Manifold absolute pressure sensors gauge intake manifold pressure, while mass air flow sensors assess incoming air volume. Coolant temperature sensors monitor engine temperature for effective cooling, and knock sensors detect engine knock, enabling adjustments for smoother operation, collectively improving vehicle performance and emissions control.</p></p>
