<p><p>The Soy Lecithin market is primarily segmented into Deoiled Lecithin and Fluid Lecithin. In contrast, Fluid Lecithin retains its natural oil content, providing excellent emulsifying properties and enhancing texture, making it ideal for use in sauces, dressings, and spreads.
