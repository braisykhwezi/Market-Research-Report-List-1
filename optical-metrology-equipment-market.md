<p><ul><li>Contact Equipment</li><li>Contactless Equipment</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The optical metrology equipment market is divided into contact and contactless equipment types. Contact equipment physically interacts with the item being measured, providing high precision but may risk damage or alteration of the sample. In contrast, contactless equipment measures without physical contact, using techniques like laser scanning or optical imaging, which preserves the integrity of the sample and allows for faster measurements. Both types play crucial roles in various industries, including manufacturing, aerospace, and automotive sectors, ensuring quality control and precision.</p></p>
