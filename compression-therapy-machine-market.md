<p><p>The compression therapy machine market is categorized into three main types: leg pressure therapy, arm pressure therapy, and whole body pressure therapy. Leg pressure therapy focuses on enhancing circulation in the lower limbs, helping reduce swelling and discomfort. Arm pressure therapy targets the upper extremities, facilitating lymphatic drainage and promoting recovery. Whole body pressure therapy encompasses the entire body, delivering uniform pressure to improve overall circulation, reduce fatigue, and aid in rehabilitation. Each type serves distinct therapeutic needs for users.</p></p>
