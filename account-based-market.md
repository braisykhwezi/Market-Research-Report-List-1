<p><p>Account-Based Marketing (ABM) is categorized into three main types: Strategic ABM, ABM Lite, and Programmatic ABM. Strategic ABM targets a small number of high-value accounts with highly personalized campaigns tailored to each account's needs. ABM Lite focuses on clusters of accounts, using more simplified, semi-customized marketing efforts. Programmatic ABM leverages automated tools to target a larger audience, using data-driven approaches to engage multiple accounts simultaneously. Each type offers varying degrees of customization and scale, catering to different marketing strategies.</p></p>
