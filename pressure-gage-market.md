<p><ul><li>Absolute Pressure Type</li><li>Gauge Pressure Type</li><li>Vacuum Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pressure gauge market comprises three primary types: Absolute Pressure Type, Gauge Pressure Type, and Vacuum Type. The Absolute Pressure Type measures pressure relative to a complete vacuum, providing precise readings in various applications. The Gauge Pressure Type measures pressure relative to atmospheric pressure, making it ideal for applications where absolute pressure is not necessary. The Vacuum Type specifically measures pressures below atmospheric levels, essential for processes requiring low pressure conditions. Each type serves distinct industries and operational needs.</p></p>
