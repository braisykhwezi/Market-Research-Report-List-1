<p><strong>The Portable Neutron Detector Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Gas Proportional Detector</li><li>Scintillation Neutron Detector</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The portable neutron detector market comprises primarily two types: gas proportional detectors and scintillation neutron detectors. Gas proportional detectors utilize low-pressure gas filled chambers to detect neutrons through ionization and are known for their sensitivity and high detection efficiency. Scintillation neutron detectors, on the other hand, use scintillation materials that emit light when struck by neutrons, enabling quick detection and measurement of neutron radiation. Both types serve critical roles in various applications, including nuclear monitoring, radiation safety, and research.</p></p>
