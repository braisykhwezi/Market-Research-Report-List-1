<p><strong>The X-Ray Flat Panel Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Indirect Type</li><li>Direct Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The X-Ray Flat Panel Sensor market is categorized into two main types: indirect and direct. Indirect flat panel sensors utilize a two-step process where X-rays are first converted into light, which is then transformed into an electrical signal. This type generally provides high sensitivity and image quality. In contrast, direct flat panel sensors convert X-rays directly into electrical signals, offering faster imaging and enhanced efficiency. Both types are pivotal in modern radiography, affecting performance and adoption in clinical settings.</p></p>
