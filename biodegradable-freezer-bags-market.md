<p><p>The biodegradable freezer bags market encompasses various types, including Poly Lactic Acid (PLA), which is derived from corn starch and compostable. Polybutylene Succinate (PBS) offers good flexibility and thermal stability while being biodegradable. Cellulose-based bags leverage natural cellulose fibers for eco-friendliness. Additionally, “Others” encompasses emerging materials and blends that enhance biodegradable properties, catering to diverse consumer preferences for sustainable packaging.</p></p>
