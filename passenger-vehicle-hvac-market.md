<p><p>The Passenger Vehicle HVAC market is primarily divided into two types: Manual HVAC and Automatic HVAC systems. Manual HVAC requires the driver to manually adjust temperature and airflow settings, offering basic climate control. In contrast, Automatic HVAC systems utilize sensors to automatically regulate the cabin temperature based on user preferences and external conditions, providing enhanced comfort and convenience. The choice between these systems often depends on vehicle type, consumer preferences, and the level of technology integration desired.</p></p>
