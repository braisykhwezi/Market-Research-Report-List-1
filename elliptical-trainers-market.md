<p><strong>The Elliptical Trainers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Front Drive</li><li>Rear Drive</li><li>Center Drive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The elliptical trainer market is categorized into three main types: front drive, rear drive, and center drive. Front drive ellipticals place the flywheel at the front, offering a more pronounced incline and engaging upper body muscles effectively. Rear drive models have the flywheel at the back, providing a smoother, more natural motion and typically lower step height. Center drive ellipticals feature a compact design with the flywheel in the center, promoting a balanced and stable workout experience while occupying less space.</p></p>
