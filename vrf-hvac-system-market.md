<p><ul><li>Horizontal Branch Pipe</li><li>Vertical Branch Pipe</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The VRF HVAC system market is categorized into horizontal and vertical branch pipe systems, each facilitating efficient refrigerant distribution. Horizontal branch pipes are typically used in low-rise buildings, allowing for easier installation and maintenance across multiple zones. In contrast, vertical branch pipes are favored in high-rise structures, delivering refrigerant upward to various floors efficiently. Both types ensure optimal climate control and energy efficiency, catering to diverse architectural designs and spatial requirements in modern construction.</p></p>
