<p><p>The Outdoor Lightning Arrester market comprises various types designed for effective lightning protection. Rod Lightning Arresters utilize a pointed rod to intercept lightning strikes, directing the discharge safely to the ground. Horn Gap Lightning Arresters feature two electrodes separated by an air gap, creating a path for lightning to safely dissipate. Multi Gap Lightning Arresters incorporate multiple gaps to enhance the capture and diversion of lightning, improving protection capabilities. Each type serves distinct applications in safeguarding infrastructures against lightning damage.</p></p>
