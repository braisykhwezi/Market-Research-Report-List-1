<p><strong>The Polyurethane Floor Coating Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Solvent Polyurethane Floor Coating</li><li>Non-Solvent Polyurethane Floor Coating</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The polyurethane floor coating market is divided into two main types: solvent-based and non-solvent-based coatings. Solvent polyurethane floor coatings contain organic solvents, providing durability and resistance, making them suitable for high-traffic areas. However, they emit volatile organic compounds (VOCs). Non-solvent polyurethane coatings, often water-based, offer low VOC emissions, making them more environmentally friendly. They provide a safer application process and faster drying times, catering to sectors focused on sustainability and health without compromising performance.</p></p>
