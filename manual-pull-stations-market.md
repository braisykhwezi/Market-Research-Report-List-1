<p><p>Manual pull stations are essential components in fire alarm systems, allowing individuals to manually activate alarms during emergencies. The market for these stations is divided into two main types: single action and dual action. Single action pull stations require only one motion, typically pulling a lever, to trigger the alarm. In contrast, dual action pull stations necessitate two actions, such as pressing a button and pulling a lever, providing an added layer of security and reducing false alarms.</p></p>
