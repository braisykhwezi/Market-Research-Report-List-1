<p><p>The ISO 5199 pumps market comprises two primary types: mag-drive pumps and sealed pumps. Mag-drive pumps utilize magnetic coupling to eliminate shaft seals, reducing leakage and maintenance needs, making them ideal for handling hazardous or aggressive fluids. In contrast, sealed pumps employ mechanical seals to maintain fluid containment, offering reliability for various applications. Both types cater to industries requiring high-performance pumps that comply with stringent safety and environmental regulations, emphasizing efficiency and durability in demanding operational environments.</p></p>
