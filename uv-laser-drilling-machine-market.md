<p><ul><li>Pulsed Laser Drilling Machine</li><li>Continuous Laser Drilling Machine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The UV laser drilling machine market features two primary types: pulsed laser drilling machines and continuous laser drilling machines. Pulsed laser drilling machines utilize short bursts of high-intensity laser energy, allowing for precise and controlled drilling of intricate patterns and small holes in materials. In contrast, continuous laser drilling machines deliver a steady beam of laser energy, ideal for high-speed operations and larger-scale drilling applications. Both technologies are essential in various industries, including electronics, aerospace, and medical devices.</p></p>
