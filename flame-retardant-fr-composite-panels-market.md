<p><strong>The Flame Retardant (FR) Composite Panels Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>A1 Grade</li><li>A2 Grade</li><li>B Grade</li><li>C Grade</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Flame retardant (FR) composite panels are categorized based on their reaction to fire, classified into A1, A2, B, and C grades. A1 grade panels offer the highest fire resistance, being non-combustible. A2 grade panels are also low in combustibility but may include limited combustible content. B grade panels provide moderate fire resistance and are typically used in less stringent applications. C grade panels have the lowest fire performance, suitable for interior environments with minimal fire risk concerns.</p></p>
