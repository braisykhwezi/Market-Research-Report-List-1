<p><ul><li>Low Flux Membrane</li><li>High Flux Membrane</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Polyethersulfone (PES) dialyzers market is segmented into low flux and high flux membrane types. Low flux membranes are designed for removing small molecules and are typically used in patients with stable kidney function. Conversely, high flux membranes facilitate the removal of larger molecules and are beneficial for patients with higher toxin levels, promoting enhanced clearance. The choice between these membranes depends on the specific clinical requirements, patient condition, and desired efficacy of dialysis treatment.</p></p>
