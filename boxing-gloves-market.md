<p><p>The boxing gloves market comprises three main types: bag gloves, sparring gloves, and competition gloves. Bag gloves are designed for heavy bag training, offering excellent padding and wrist support to absorb impacts. Sparring gloves, which feature more padding for safety, are used during training with partners, ensuring both fighters are protected. Competition gloves are lighter with less padding, allowing for greater punch speed and precision; they are specifically crafted for official bouts, adhering to regulatory standards.</p></p>
