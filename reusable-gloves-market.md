<p><p>The reusable gloves market comprises several types, each suited for specific applications. Nitrile gloves are synthetic, providing puncture resistance and chemical protection. PVC gloves are cost-effective and suitable for light-duty tasks, though they lack flexibility. Butyl rubber gloves excel in chemical resistance and durability, while polyurethane (PU) gloves combine flexibility with strength, making them ideal for various industrial and medical applications.</p></p>
