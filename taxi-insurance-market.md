<p><strong>The Taxi Insurance Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Third Party</li><li>Third party Fire and Theft</li><li>Comprehensive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Taxi insurance market offers three primary types: </p><p>1. **Third Party**: This covers damages to other vehicles and injuries to other people in accidents caused by the insured taxi driver but does not cover the driver or their vehicle.</p><p>2. **Third Party Fire and Theft**: This includes all third-party coverage plus protection against theft and fire damage to the taxi.</p><p>3. **Comprehensive**: This provides complete coverage, including liability for other parties, as well as damage to the taxi itself from accidents, theft, and other incidents.</p></p>
